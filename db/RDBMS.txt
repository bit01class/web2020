데이터베이스 관리 시스템(database management system, DBMS)
관계형 데이터베이스 관리 시스템(relational database management system, RDBMS)

SQL(Structured Query Language, 구조화 질의어, S-Q-L)

1. DML (Data Manipullation Language)	- commit,rollback
	a.insert (Create)
	b.select (Read) - DQL
	c.update (Update)
	d.delete (Delete)
2. DDL (Date Definition Language) - auto commit[컬럼명변경시는 commit해야함]
	a.create
	b.alter
	c.drop
	d.rename
	e.truncate
3. DCL (Data Control Language)
	a.grant
	b.revoke
4. TCL (Transaction Control Language)
	a.commit
	b.rollback
	c.savepoint
